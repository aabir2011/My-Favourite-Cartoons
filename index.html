Sony Yay! is an Indian pay television channel aimed at children, operated by Sony Pictures Networks India. The channel replaced Animax in 2017.

Pogo TV is an Indian cable and satellite television channel owned by AT&T's WarnerMedia under its International division as a part of the CN brand. The network, which primarily airs animated programming, is based in Mumbai, Maharashtra.
